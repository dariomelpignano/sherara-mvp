# Sherara MVP - Sample Policy Document

## Data Protection and Privacy Policy

### 1. Introduction
This document outlines our organization's commitment to protecting personal data and ensuring compliance with applicable data protection regulations. We recognize the importance of privacy and take our responsibilities seriously.

### 2. Data Collection and Processing

#### 2.1 Lawful Basis
We process personal data only when we have a lawful basis:
- Consent from the data subject
- Contractual necessity
- Legal obligations
- Legitimate interests

#### 2.2 Data Minimization
We collect only the minimum personal data necessary for specified purposes. Data collection is limited to what is relevant and necessary.

### 3. Data Subject Rights

#### 3.1 Access Rights
Individuals have the right to request access to their personal data. We will provide a copy of the data within one month of request.

#### 3.2 Rectification
Data subjects can request correction of inaccurate personal data.

#### 3.3 Erasure
Under certain circumstances, individuals may request deletion of their personal data.

### 4. Security Measures

#### 4.1 Technical Measures
We implement appropriate technical measures including:
- Encryption of sensitive data
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security assessments

#### 4.2 Organizational Measures
Our organizational measures include:
- Staff training on data protection
- Data protection policies and procedures
- Regular reviews and updates

### 5. Data Breach Response

In the event of a personal data breach, we will:
- Assess the risk to individuals
- Notify supervisory authorities within 72 hours if required
- Inform affected individuals if high risk

### 6. International Transfers

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international transfers of personal data.

### 7. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.

### 8. Governance

#### 8.1 Data Protection Officer
We have appointed a Data Protection Officer responsible for overseeing compliance.

#### 8.2 Privacy by Design
We incorporate data protection considerations into all new projects and processes.

### 9. Contact Information

For any questions about this policy or to exercise your rights, please contact our Data Protection Officer.
